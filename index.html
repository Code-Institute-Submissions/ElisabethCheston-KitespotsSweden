Are you a kitesurfers (or windsurfer/surfer) traveling to/or live in Sweden?

This site will provide you with all the current functional kitespots in Sweden, open to the public. Also present a couple of kitesurfing communities.

"Research A KITESPOT"

Select your spot in the "CHOOSE A KITESPOT" window. The page will zoom in to the spot and you can research the surrounding.

LAYER CONTROL

The Layer Control (right under "CHOOSE A KITESPOT") has different map view to easier analyze launging area/surrounding/water dept/etc.
You can also see layers for the regions the spots are locaded in and the cities in them.

Links & Communities in Sweden

There are many communities and business for kitesurfing in Sweden. At the bottom of the page you find links to three that will help you get in contact with fellow surfers.

(Hidden Home Button)
