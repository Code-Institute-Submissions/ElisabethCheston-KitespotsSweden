This webpage will show ALL current functional kitespots locations in Sweden!

Are you a kitesurfers (or windsurfer/surfer) traveling to/or live in Sweden, this site will provide you with all the current functional kitespots thats open to public in Sweden?

"CHOOSE A KITESPOT"

By selecting a spot you like to research and the page will zoom in to its position.

LAYER CONTROL

In the Layer Control (right under "CHOOSE A KITESPOT") you can view the selected spot in different map views to easier analyze launging area/surrounding/water dept/etc. options.
